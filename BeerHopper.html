BeerHopper

The Project

Beer Hopper is an interactive tourist ipad application designed to help people navigate their way throughout the bars of a city in search of craft beer. It also supplyies the user with a valuable look inside a bar's personality (live action) along with information on the craft beers that are served.

This was created for a Collaborative Class at SCAD, where I worked with Motion Designers and Filmmakers for a Beer Tour Application of Savannah.

Skills: Object-C, Xcode, Wireframing, Human-centered, Information Design
