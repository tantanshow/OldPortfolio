About Me

Bio:

Hsiutan Hsiao(Tan) is currently a UX researcher/designer at BrightStarr with 4 years User Experience. She is extremely well versed across all responsive web design and mobile apps, doing so in a lean or agile work environment. Tan has worked with a number of high profile Fortune 500 clients including: Apple, Delta, YUM Brands (KFC/Pizza Hut/Taco Bell), Hess, Bain Capital, Mecklenburg County, and the United States Postal Service. One of her most recent projects, for Mecklenburg County, was nominated for the Webby Awards. She has a passion for User Experience with a focus on leveraging easy-to-use interface and product design to bring technology and people closer. Tan is an experienced at bringing vague project ideas to practical fruition by applying her background in User-Centered Design, Interaction Design, Software Engineering and Quality Assurance.

About Me:

I came from Taiwan to America four years ago. I have a M.A. master degree in the interactive design and game development program at the Savannah college of Art and Design. Owing to living in different cultures, I like to work with different kinds of people and new interesting things. Even more, I never give up. For example, I have been able reach my goal of losing 66 lbs in 6 months through hard work and exercise, but I have not given up, I will continue to strengthen not only my physical self, but also my mental abilities.
