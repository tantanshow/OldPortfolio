WW3

The Project

This game combines the elements of crossword puzzles and Chinese Mahjong. Now I turned this game into something for everyone and every generation, something everyone could play. This is a game using skill, strategy, calculation, and a little luck. It's a game of English vocabulary learning. The game will keep you wanting to play as you learn English vocabulary.
